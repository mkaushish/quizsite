*** Day 1
*** Ijaz Ahmed's 5th test century. First by a Pakistani at Headingley.
*** Ijaz Ahmed was caught by Cork off a no-ball from Caddick when on 66, and
    Pakistan total at 113/3.
*** When on 138, Ijaz Ahmed crossed his previous highest test score of 137
    scored against Australia at Sydney, 1995-96.

*** Day 2
*** Moin Khan dropped at long-leg by Mullally off Caddick, when on 8.
*** Moin Khan caught by Stewart off a no-ball from Mullally when on 18.
*** Moin Khan dropped by Cork off his own bowling.
*** Moin Khan's 3rd test century, first against England.
*** Asif Mujtaba and Moin Khan set a record 7th wicket partnership of 122
    runs for Pakistan against England. The previous best was 89 runs by Ijaz
    Ahmed and Salim Yousuf at The Oval in 1987.
*** Russell picked up his 150th dismissal when he caught Moin Khan off the
    bowling of Cork.
*** 3rd 5-wicket haul for Cork.
*** Rain stopped play for 15 minutes with Pakistan 444-9.
*** Highest score by Pakistan at Headingley.

*** Day 3
*** Rain delays start of play by 60 minutes.
*** Stewart's 8th test century, second against Pakistan.
*** Stewart dropped by Wasim Akram off his own bowling when on 152, and
    England Total 329/4.
*** 170 is the second highest score by Stewart. His highest score of 190 was
    also scored against Pakistan at Edgbaston in 1992.
*** Bad light stopped the play.

*** Day 4
*** Start of play delayed by 1 hour due to wet outfield.
*** Maiden test century for Knight.
*** Rain stops the play at 473/9 in 147.2 overs. Early Tea taken.

*** Players came onto bat for the Pakistani innings, but went back due to
    poor light.
*** Rain stops further play.

*** Day 5
*** Shadab Kabir dropped by Thorpe at slip off Mullaly from the first ball he
    recieved.
*** Third replacement ball (original out of shape) after 19.1 overs of the
    Pakistan 2nd innings.
*** Saleem Malik reached 5001 runs in test cricket when he got to 6 runs with
    a boundary off Caddick. He became the third Pakistani batsman to cross
    5000 runs after Zaheer Abbas and Javed Miandad.
*** Ijaz Ahmed dropped by Hussain off Lewis, when on 32 and Pakistan total at
    142/4.
*** Atherton picks up his second test wicket in his career.
*** Pakistan declared at 5.20pm.

*** Man of the Match adjudicator: Mushtaq Mohammad
