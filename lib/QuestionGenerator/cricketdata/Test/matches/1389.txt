*** Day 3
*** Wallace kept wicket for West Indies in place of Williams who did not take
    his place on the field (low blood pressure).

*** Inzaman-ul-Haq and Aamer Sohail set a new partnership record for Pakistan
    v West Indies of 323; the previous best of 169 was set in Test 2,
    Port-of-Spain, 1958 between Saeed Ahmed and Wazir Mohammed.
