*** Day 3
*** Lunch: Eng 210/8 (Atherton 92*, Caddick 4*)
*** MA Atherton carried his bat through the innings. This was the 8th time by
    an Englishman and the 35th occasion in Test history, the most recent
    being MH Dekker for Zimbabwe against Pakistan at Rawalpindi in 1993/94.
*** BA Young dropped by Knight off Cork when 0
*** Young was reported to the match referee for dissent after he refused to
    go when being given out. The match referee accepted his word that he
    didn't see the umpire giving him out.

*** Day 4
*** Lunch: NZ 186 (Vettori 29*)
*** Tea: Eng 71/1 (Atherton 35*, Stewart 1*)
*** CL Cairns brought up 1000 Test runs upon scoring his 50.

*** Day 5
*** AR Caddick was given not out by DB Hair after trapping the ball between
    his pad and bat and then being caught by a close-in fielder. TV replays
    showed that the catch was fair

*** Only the 2nd time England have chased more than 300 to win a Test, the
    other being 7/332 against Australia in 1928/29. It was the 15th time by
    any team, the most recent being Pakistan 9/315 against Australia at
    Karachi in 1994/95.
*** The first time England have won consecutive Tests since a run of 4 wins
    in a row against West Indies, Sri Lanka, and 2 against New Zealand from
    Aug 1991 - Feb 1992.
*** England's first overseas series win since 1991/92 in the corresponding
    series against New Zealand
*** England finish their 5-Test winter season unbeaten. The last time this
    occured for 5 or more Tests was in 1970/71 when they were unbeaten in 8
    Tests against Australia and New Zealand
