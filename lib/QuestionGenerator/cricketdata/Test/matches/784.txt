*** Pak 1st innings
*** Majid scored 100 before lunch on the first day (112 mins, 77 balls),
    emulating Trumper, Macartney, and Bradman
*** Miandad (410 mins, 2 sixes, 29 fours) youngest to score a double-century
    in a test -- aged 19 years, 141 days.
*** Pakistan innings of 565 was the record for any test in Karachi

*** NZ 1st innings
*** Lees first century in first-class cricket, first by a NZ 'keeper in a
    test
*** Lees/Hadlee partnership of 186 new seventh-wicket record for NZ
*** Imran was banned from bowling for the rest of the innings after being
    warned by both umpires for bowling too many short-pitched balls

*** Highest aggregate of runs (1585) for any test on the Indian sub-continent
    up to that time
