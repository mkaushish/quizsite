*** South Africa's first home Test since March 1970.
*** Omar Henry, the first black player to appear for South Africa, becomes
    the oldest debutant for South Africa (40 years & 295 days).

*** RSA 1st inn:
*** SJ Cook (making his debut at the age of 39 years) the first man to be
    dismissed on the first ball of his debut Test.
*** KC Wessels becomes the first man to score centuries for 2 countries.

*** Ind 1st inn:
*** SR Tendulkar becomes the first Test player to be dismissed by TV replay.
