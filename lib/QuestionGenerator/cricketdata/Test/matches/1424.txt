*** Day 1
*** Lunch: Pakistan 78/4 (Saeed Anwar 35*, Azhar Mahmood 10*, 27 overs)
*** Tea: Pakistan 171/8 (Saeed Anwar 81*, Mushtaq Ahmed 0*, 59 overs)
*** On reaching 2, Inzamam ul-Haq scored his 3000th Test run.
*** IA Healy's catch of Mohammad Wasim was his 355th dismissal in Tests,
    equalling RW Marsh's world record for wicketkeepers.
*** CR Miller dismissed Saleem Malik with his 5th delivery in Test cricket.
*** Rain stopped play at 4.39pm

*** Day 2
*** Play commences at 9.42am to make up time from day 1.
*** Lunch: Pakistan 269, Australia 55/3 (Slater 36*, SR Waugh 9*, 28 overs)
*** Tea: Australia 150/3 (Slater 72*, SR Waugh 66*, 53 overs)
*** Mushtaq Ahmed/Saeed Anwar 120 partnership: Pakistan 9th wicket record v
    Australia (prev. 56, Intikhab Alam & Afaq Hussain, Melbourne 1964).

*** Day 3
*** Lunch: Australia 352/4 (SR Waugh 157*, Lehmann 61*, 113 overs)
*** Tea: Australia 428/5 (Lehmann 97*, Healy 36*, 143 overs)

*** Day 4
*** Lunch: Pakistan 55/4 (Saleem Malik 9*, Moin Khan 12*, 30 overs)
*** Tea: Pakistan 111/7 (Saleem Malik 38*, Mohammad Hussain 5*, 59 overs)
*** Wasim Akram was Healy's 356th dismissal in Tests, the most in Tests by a
    wicketkeeper (prev. 355, RW Marsh 1970/71-1983/84).
*** Rain stopped play at 2.27pm local time. Tea was taken and play resumed at
    4.54pm. Play was extended until bad light stopped play at 5.30pm.

*** Day 5
*** Play started 30 minutes early due to time lost on Day 4
*** Match finished at 9:43am

*** 3rd victory by Australia in Tests in Pakistan (prev. 1st Test at Dacca,
    1959/60; 2nd Test at Lahore, 1959/60).
*** 3rd innings loss by Pakistan in Tests in Pakistan. (prev. 3rd Test v West
    Indies at Lahore, 1958/59; 2nd Test v West Indies at Lahore, 1986/87).
