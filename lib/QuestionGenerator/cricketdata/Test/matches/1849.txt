*** Anil Kumble is the 30th player to captain India in Tests.
*** When Dravid caught Salman Butt in the second innings, it was his 50th
    catch off Kumble, making them only the third fielder-bowler combination
    in Test history to claim at least 50 dismissals.
*** Sachin Tendulkar went past Allan Border's aggregate of 11,174 to become
    the second-highest run-getter in Tests. His aggregate at the end of the
    match was 11,207.
*** India have won ten Tests at the Feroz Shah Kotla, seven of them in a row.
    Only at the MA Chidambaram Stadium have they won more (11 Tests).
