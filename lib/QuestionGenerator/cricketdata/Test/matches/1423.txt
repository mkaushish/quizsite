*** Day 2
*** Crawley and Fraser's partnership beats the previous record for England v
    Sri Lanka (40, between Malcolm and Emburey in 1993).

*** Day 3
*** When de Silva reached 23, he became the first Sri Lankan to reach 5000
    Test runs
*** de Silva and Jayasuriya's stand of 243 was the highest for any Sri Lankan
    wicket against England, or for their third wicket against any country.

*** Day 5
*** UDU Chandana substituted for PA de Silva
*** MS Atapattu scored his 1000th Test run
*** Muralitharan took his 200th Test wicket when he dismissed Cork
*** Muralitharan's 9/65 was the best bowling in Tests by a Sri Lankan, and
    the 7th best analysis of all time in Tests
*** Muralitharan's match figures of 16/220 are the best by a Sri Lankan, and
    the 5th best analysis of all time in Tests
