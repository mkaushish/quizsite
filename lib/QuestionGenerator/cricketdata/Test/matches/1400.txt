*** Day 1
*** Lunch: SA 75/2 (25 overs)
*** Tea: SA 160/7 (50 overs)
*** Hudson dropped on 8, Pollock dropped on 10, Boucher dropped on 23
*** Symcox and Boucher break the 9th wicket partnership for SA at The
    Wanderers

*** Day 2
*** Lunch: Pak 1/0 (Aamer Sohail 0*, Saeed Anwar 0*, 3 overs)
*** Boucher/Symcox 195 partnership, South African 9th wicket record in all
    Tests (prev 137, EL Dalton & ABC Langton v England at The Oval, 1935),
    world record for 9th wicket (prev. 190, Asif Iqbal & Intikhab Alam,
    Pakistan v England at The Oval, 1967)
*** PS Symcox 108:
*** Highest Test score
*** The third batsman in Test history to score a century from number ten in
    the batting order. (Others: WW Read 117, England v Australia at The Oval,
    1884; RA Duff, 104 on debut, Australia v England at Melbourne, 1901-02)

*** Day 3
*** Lunch: Pak 171/5 (Moin Khan 31*, Azhar Mahmood 29*, 47 overs)

*** Azhar Mahmood's 136 is his highest Test score
*** 21 overs lost to bad light and rain during the day
*** Alan Donald becomes highest wicket taker for SA at The Wanderers
