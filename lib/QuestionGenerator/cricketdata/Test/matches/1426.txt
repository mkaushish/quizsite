*** Day 2
*** Play commenced at 9.30am (30 minutes early) due to time lost on Day 1.
    Lunch taken at 12.30pm due to Friday prayers.
*** Lunch: Australia 388/2 (Taylor 215*, ME Waugh 29*, 112 overs)
*** Tea: Australia 458/4 (Taylor 253*, Ponting 17*, 135 overs)
*** Taylor/Langer, 270 partnership for 2nd wicket: Australian record
    partnership for any wicket against Pakistan (prev. 259 for 2nd wicket, WB
    Phillips/GN Yallop at Perth, 1983/84)
*** Taylor scored 113 runs before lunch (180 min session)
*** On 210 Taylor became the fourth highest run scorer for Australia in Tests
    (passing DG Bradman 6996)
*** On 213 Taylor passed 7000 Test career runs, the 16th batsman to do so.
*** On 16 ME Waugh became the 10th highest run scorer for Australia in Tests
    (passing WM Lawry, 5234)
*** On 324 Taylor became the third highest run scorer for Australia in Tests
    (passing GS Chappell, 7110)
*** On 334 Taylor equalled the highest individual score by an Australian in
    Tests (also DG Bradman 334 v England, Leeds 1930)
*** Taylor scored 222 runs on Day 2.
