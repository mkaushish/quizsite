*** Day 1
*** SA 2nd wicket partnership equals the record for South Africa. Previously
    212 by G Kirsten and DJ Cullinan vs India at Calcutta, 1996/97.
*** It is also a new record at Old Trafford. The previous record was 200 by
    IM Barrow and GA Headly for West Indies, 1933.

*** Day 2
*** South Africa set a new 2nd wicket partnership of 238 against England.
*** JH Kallis makes his highest test score, his previous best was 101.
*** G Kirsten makes his highest test score, his previous best of 133 was made
    at Calcutta in 1996, it is also the longest innings for a South African,
    the previous best was 9 hrs 35 mins by Jackie McGlew at Durban in 1958
*** Both Kallis and Kirsten have beaten the highest score by a South African
    at Old Trafford.

*** Day 3
*** South Africa make their highest score against England.
