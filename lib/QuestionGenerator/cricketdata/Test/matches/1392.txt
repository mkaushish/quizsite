*** Day 1
*** Lunch: Australia 44/3 (Blewett 13*; 28.4 overs)
*** Tea: Australia 92/4 (SR Waugh 28*, Ponting 5*; 59 overs)
*** Elliott dropped by Kallis off Donald in the 1st over.
*** Richardson took the South African record for Most Fielding Dismissals
    apon catching Elliott (previous: JHB Waite, 141 Dismissals).
*** SR Waugh was caught by Richardson off a no-ball by Donald soon after
    reaching his 50.
*** SR Waugh was dropped by Kirsten in 2nd last over of the day.

*** Day 2
*** Lunch: Australia 302/7 (Reiffel 21*; 117.5 overs)
*** Tea: South Africa 41/1 (Kirsten 31*, Kallis 5*; 22 overs)

*** Day 3
*** Lunch: South Africa 147/5 (McMillan 31*, Pollock 3*; 87 overs)
*** Tea: Australia 8/1 (Taylor 2*, Blewett 4*; 8 overs)
*** The dismissal of Pollock was SK Warne's 500th First-class wicket

*** Day 4
*** Lunch: Australia 146/8 (Reiffel 8*, Kasprowicz 0*; 65 overs)
*** Tea: Australia 257 all out 96.2 overs
*** With the dismissal of Kasprowicz, Donald passes the highest number of
    Test wickets taken in a career by a South African (170, HJ Tayfield)
*** Paul Reiffel passes his highest Test score
*** With the dismissal of Kirsten, Reiffel claims his 100th Test wicket
*** With the score on 2/0 AM Bacher was given out to a catch by RT Ponting,
    Ponting then advises that the catch was grounded and the decision was
    reversed.

*** Day 5
*** Lunch: South Africa 139/3 (Kallis 67*, Cronje 27*; 60 overs)
*** Tea: South Africa 226/4 (Kallis 100*, McMillan 6*; 93 overs)
*** Jacques Kallis passes his highest Test score
