*** Day 1
*** Gary Kirsten plays his 50th consecutive Test
*** Mark Boucher scores his maiden Test century
*** Courtney Walsh takes his 16th 5 wicket haul in an innings
*** Reifer drops Klusener (10) in the slips
*** Paid attendance 6,890

*** Day 2
*** Allan Donald takes his 17th 5 wicket haul in an innings
*** Courtney Walsh leaves the field with a suspected hamstring injury
*** Nixon McLean completes the over
*** Paid attendance 14,305

*** Day 3
*** Courtney Walsh is ruled out of the rest of the match with a strained
    hamstring - replaced by Raul Lewis on the field
*** Gary Kirsten scores his 8th Test century, 2nd with B. Mitchell and 1
    behind A.D Nourse
*** Jacques Kallis joins some greats in scoring 450 runs and taking 15
    wickets in a 5 match test series
*** Jonty Rhodes scores 2,000 Test runs and his 3rd Test century which is the
    fastest Test century in South African cricket off 95 balls
*** Paid attendance 17,500 (capacity crowd)

*** Day 4
*** Ridley Jacobs (78) scores his highest test score
*** Ridley Jacobs (317) passes Brian Lara (310) as the top West Indies scorer
    in the series
*** Largest defeat, by runs, of any West Indies team
*** The 1st time the West Indies have lost a 5 match series 5-0
