*** England's total of 77 is their lowest at Lord's since 1888, their lowest

    1993-94.
*** GD McGrath 8-38:
*** * His personal best bowling in Test and first-class cricket;
*** * The 23rd best individual bowling by any player in Test cricket;
*** * The 3rd best innings performance by an Australian in a Test. (Best
    since Arthur Mailey took 9-121 v England at Melbourne, 1920-21);
*** * The best individual bowling by an Australian in an innings at Lord's
    (previously 8-53 by Bob Massie, 1972);
*** * The best individual bowling for either side in an Ashes Test at Lord's
    (previously Hedley Verity, 1934);
*** * The second-best bowling in an innings of a Lord's Test (best 8-34 by
    Ian Botham v Pakistan, 1978</a>).
