*** Day 1
*** Hansie Cronje breaks John Waite's test appearance record for South
    Africa.

    in England.
*** Mark Butcher scores his maiden test hundred.

*** Day 2
*** England dropped 4 catches, 3 in 23 balls. Cronje and Rhodes were each
    dropped twice, the culprits being Ramprakash, Hussain, Hick and Stewart.
*** Fraser's 3rd successive five wicket innings.
