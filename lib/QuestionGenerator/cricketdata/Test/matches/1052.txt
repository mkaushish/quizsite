*** Gavaskar became the first played to make 100 consecutive Test

*** Jones's 210 was the highest score by an Australian in a Test in India. It
    took 503 minutes, 330 balls and included 27 fours and 2 sixes. He had to
    be treated in hospital after the completion of the innings for
    exhaustion.

*** More suffered food poisoning and left the field after 50 minutes play on
    the first morning. Pandit deputising as wicket-keeper.
