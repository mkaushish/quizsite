*** Day 1
*** Botham takes 300 wickets, and becomes the first to complete the 'triple
    double' of 3000 runs and 300 wkts.

*** Day 2
*** G Fowler (1) retired hurt on 12* from 21/1 to 83/5 (struck on the arm by
    a Marshall delivery)

*** Day 5
*** PI Pocock becomes the second Englishman after R.Peel (1894-5) to be
    dismissed for a `pair' twice in consecutive matches.

*** This is the 5th 5-Test blackwash in Test history, and the first to occur
    in England.
