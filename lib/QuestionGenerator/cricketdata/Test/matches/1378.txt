*** First time 3 pairs of brothers have appeared on the same team in a Test
    (A & GW Flower, PA & BC Strang, JA & GJ Rennie).

*** Day 1
*** 1 hour and 15 minutes were lost to rain on the first morning

*** Day 2
*** SP Fleming 5 catches, equals the record for most catches by a fielder in
    a Test innings (also: VY Richardson, Aus v SA at Durban, 5th test,
    1935/36; Yajurvindra Singh, Ind v Eng at Bangalore, 4th Test, 1976/77; M
    Azharuddin, Ind v Pak at Karachi, 1st Test, 1989/90; K Srikkanth, Ind v
    Aus at Perth, 5th Test, 1991/92); new Test record for New Zealand
    (previous - 4 catches: JJ Crowe v WI at Bridgetown, 3rd Test, 1984/85; MD
    Crowe v WI at Kingston, 4th Test, 1984/85).

*** Day 4
*** GW Flower becomes the first player to score a century in each innings of
    a Test for Zimbabwe.
*** GJ Rennie and GW Flower's 156 set a new record partnership for the 1st
    wicket for Zimbabwe in Tests (previous: GW Flower and SV Carlisle,
    Zimbabwe v New Zealand at Auckland, 1995-96).
*** GW Flower (1430 runs) becomes Zimbabwe's highest Test run-scorer
    (previous: DL Houghton 1419 runs)
*** SP Fleming 7 catches in the match, equals the record for most catches by
    a fielder in a Test (also: GS Chappell, Aus v Eng at Perth, 2nd Test,
    1974/75; Yajurvindra Singh, Ind v Eng at Bangalore, 4th Test, 1976/77; HP
    Tillakaratne, SL v NZ at Colombo, 2nd Test, 1992/93); new record for New
    Zealand (previous - 6 catches: BA Young v Pak at Auckland, 1st Test,
    1993/94).
