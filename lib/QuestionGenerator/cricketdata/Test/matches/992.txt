*** PI Pocock returns after missing 86 Tests, the second longest wait for an
    Englishman. (HL Jackson missed 96)

*** Day 2
*** CG Greenidge: 223 off 425, 588 mins, 30x4 The second WI batsman to score
    2 double-centuries in the same series (the other being IVA Richards)

*** Day 3
*** VP Terry (1) retired hurt on 7* from 105/1 to 278/9 (fractured left ulna
    from a Davis delivery).

*** Day 4
*** In VP Terry's return to the wicket, he batted one-handed with his broken
    arm in a sling under his sweater, to aid Lamb (98*) reach his century.
*** AJ Lamb scores 3 consecutive hundreds in the same series, the first
    England batsman to do so since KF Barrington (v Pakistan, 1967)
