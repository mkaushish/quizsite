*** Day 1
*** The last instance when England played 2 left-arm seamers together was in
    1912, H.Dean and F.Foster, 2 Tests.
*** Simon Brown picked up his maiden wicket off his 10th ball in Tests.
*** Cork was off the ground for a brief while to get his elbow bandaged,
    Andrew Flintoff substituted.
*** Saleem Malik was dropped by Atherton (a difficult chance) of Ealham when
    he had not opened his account.
*** Inzamam-ul-Haq's 5th Test century, all of them made abroad. He reached
    his century with a 4 and a 6 off Hick's bowling.
*** Cork left the field in the 72nd over. Flintoff fielding as the
    substitute.
*** Inzamam-ul-Haq dropped by Thorpe off Brown in the slips, at the
    individual score of 132, and Pakistan Total 251/5.
*** When on 136, Inzamam-ul-Haq's crossed his previous highest score of 135*
    made against New Zealand.
*** Mushtaq Ahmed and Rashid Latif ran 5 runs, but the TV replay umpire ruled
    that as only a 4, as the ball crossed the boundary.
*** The 9th wicket fell on the 4th ball of the last over of the day. That was
    the close of play for Day 1.

*** Day 2
*** Knight edged a googly from Mushtaq Ahmed and was dropped in the 1st slip
    by Aamir Sohail, when his score was 48 and England Total 100/1. While
    Knight indicated that he had played the ball, the Umpire awarded legbyes
    (2 runs). Replays however showed that Knight had indeed hit the ball.
*** During the Tea interval, the umpires decided to award 2 runs to Knight
    (that were earlier awarded as legbyes).
*** Ealham gave a chance of caught and bowled to Mushtaq Ahmed when on 18 and
    England Total 157/4.
*** Wasim Akram off the field, Moin Khan the substitute fielder.
*** Thorpe dropped at short leg off the bowling of Mushtaq Ahmed, when on 43,
    and England Total 200/5, in the last over of the day.

*** Day 3
*** Inzamam-ul-Haq bruised his left knee and left the field. Shahid Anwar
    substituted.
*** Thorpe dropped by Rashid Latif off the bowling of Waqar Younis, when on
    73 and England Total at 254/5.
*** Lunch taken at the fall of the 9th wicket, Mullally.

*** Aamer Sohail is injured. Shadab Kabir is opening the batting with Saeed
    Anwar.
*** A female streaker runs towards the pavilion.

*** Day 4
*** Atherton dropped by Rashid Latif off Ata-ur-Rehman when on 19, and the
    England Total at 39/1.
*** A maximum of 124 overs to be bowled in England's 2nd innings.
