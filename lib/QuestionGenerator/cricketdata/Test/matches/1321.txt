*** Day 3
*** On Thorpe's run out, umpire Orchard initially gave not out
*** but consulted TV replay after discussions with Cronje, and
*** reversed his decision.
*** Tea taken early at fall of England's 10th wicket.

*** AA Donald awarded Man of the Series for 'contributing the
*** most towards South Africa winning the series'.
*** Following the presentations, commentator Jack Bannister
*** honoured his pre-series promise that he would "eat this
*** piece of paper if South Africa win the series".
*** Scheduled 5th day of Test replaced by a day/night limited
*** overs match versus Western Province.

*** South Africa's first series win at home against England since
*** 1930-31.
