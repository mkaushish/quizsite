*** SR Waugh's 150th Test

*** Day 1
*** Lunch: Pakistan 50/9 (Rashid Latif 3*, 28.4 overs)
*** Pakistan's total of 59 was their lowest in Test cricket (previous lowest:
    62 v Australia at Perth, 1981/82)
*** Rashid Latif (Hamstring strain & back soreness) did not keep in
    Australian innings, Taufeeq Umar deputised
*** Tea: Australia 58/1 (Hayden 19*, Ponting 9*, 14 overs)

*** Day 2
*** Taufeeq Umar continues to keep wicket for Rashid Latif in the Australia
    innings
*** Lunch: Australia 261/6 (Hayden 106*, Warne 2*, 76 overs)
*** Abdul Razzaq retired hurt at 11/1 (struck on wrist by Brett Lee delivery,
    5.4 overs)
*** Tea: Pakistan 12/1 (Imran Nazir 6*, Younis Khan 0*, 6 overs)
*** Younis Khan acted as runner for Rashif Latif
*** Pakistan's total of 53 surpassed their lowest total in Tests set in the
    first innings
*** This Test was the 17th occasion a match was completed in two days (first
    involving Pakistan, 11th involving Australia)
