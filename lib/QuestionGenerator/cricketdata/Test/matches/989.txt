*** TA Lloyd became the first Shropshire-born cricketer to play for England

*** Day 1
*** TA Lloyd retired hurt at 20/2 (struck on the helmet around the temple by
    a ball from Marshall). He was hospitalised for several days with blurred
    vision, and did not play again until 1985. This was also his only Test
    appearance.

*** Day 2
*** IVA Richards passes 5000 runs, the 4th WI batsman to do so.
