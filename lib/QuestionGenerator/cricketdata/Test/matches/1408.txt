*** Day 1
*** Lunch: No Play
*** Tea: Zimbabwe 102/3 (GW Flower 42*, A Flower 38*; 36 overs)
*** The start of play was delayed until the end of the lunch interval due to
    rain. Play will now be in two 2.5 hour sessions, resulting in only one
    hour's play being lost in the day.
*** Bad light stopped play at 5.00 pm and play was called off soon after.

*** Day 2
*** Lunch: Zimbabwe 228/6 (GW Flower 114*, Streak 26*; 83 overs)
*** Tea: Zimbabwe 310/8 (GW Flower 149*, AR Whittall 13*; 109 overs)
*** GW Flower became the first Zimbabwean to score five Test centuries.
*** GW Flower became the second Zimbabwean batsman to carry his bat through a
    Test innings after MH Dekker against Pakistan at Rawalpindi, 1993/94.

*** Day 3
*** Lunch: Pakistan 159/6 (Yousuf Youhana 28*, Rashif Latif 7*; 58 overs)
*** Tea: Pakistan 209/7 (Yousuf Youhana 54*, Saqlain Mushtaq 0*; 82 overs)
*** Rain stopped play for 28 minutes between 1.24 p.m. and 1.52 p.m. The time
    lost was added to the end of the days play.

*** Lunch: Zimbabwe 79/4 (Goodwin 45*, A Flower 27*; 34 overs)
*** Tea: Zimbabwe 206/4 (Goodwin 98*, A Flower 77*; 62 overs)
*** MW Goodwin's 50 came in 139 mins off 94 balls
*** A Flower became the second Zimbabwean to score five Test centuries
*** Goodwin/A Flower partnership 277*:
