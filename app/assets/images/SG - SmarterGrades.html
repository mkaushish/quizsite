FAQs

How does the school register?

  1. Send in the attached excel sheet registration template with the details as requested to sgchallenge2014@ulearntoday.com.
  2. You will receive a confirmation email from us immediately.
  3. Within 2 working days, we will also send across an excel sheet with details of the userids and passwords for your students. We will also create a teacher userid for the teacher to track individual and group performance.
  4. Do let us know if you would like us to create sections within the list of students for ease of teacher monitoring.

How does the points system work for the 3 rounds?

  1. Points will be awarded for correct answers, speed of responses, difficulty level of questions and topics covered.

Can the teachers monitor the performance of the students?

  1. Along with the userids and passwords for the students, one userid and password for teacher access would be given as well. Through this userid, the teacher can track individual and group performance of the students. It will also highlight the common errors made by students and gap in conceptual understanding of students if any.

When would the prizes be announced and given away?

  1. Winners would be announced at 8pm on [Date].
  2. The mechanism for claiming the prizes would be shared by [Date + 1 week].
